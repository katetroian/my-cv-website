002 : Telegram bot

If you do shopping once a week, it will be convenient to create a shopping list, in case you forgot something. For a long time, I have been using “Notes” at the phone, but lists always lost among the others, so, I decided to create structured and well-looked helper to facilitate daily routine. Up to date, Shopping list bot is trained to create/update name/delete lists and add/update quantity/delete items in the list. Granted, the bot checks for matches all inputs in the database just not to repeat it. In addition, it checks user permission by the user’s id in Telegram and new member has ability to send access request through the bot to the admin with necessary information.
