001 : Image parser

One of the Glovo’s partner did not have opportunity to share content from their website. I created a parser that found images by attribute and saved them with the name of the product exactly as on the website. Thereafter all files were uploaded to Amazon bucket. This parser runs in 3 stages: finds and saves all product links; iterates through the csv-file, downloads image with the right name; uploads them to the bucket and writes the name and url to the general file.
